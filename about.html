About Us

Learn more about our history, values and how we work toward a fairer future for all.

Who We Are

Income Equality Initiative is a grassroots non‑profit organization founded in 2024 by activists, academics, and workers united by a shared belief: that everyone deserves a living wage and equitable opportunities. Through research, public education, and advocacy, we shine a light on income inequality and champion pro‑labor policies.

Our Values

  • Solidarity – we stand together with workers and marginalized communities.
  • Justice – we believe in fairness, dignity and the right to thrive.
  • Transparency – we operate openly and hold institutions accountable.
  • Empowerment – we empower people to make their voices heard.

Our Team

Behind our mission is a small yet passionate team of volunteers and experts. Our board comprises community organizers, economists, lawyers, educators, and labor advocates who guide our strategy and ensure we remain true to our mission.

Leadership

Our leadership team sets the vision and works tirelessly to promote systemic change.

Volunteers

Volunteers fuel our campaigns, events, and outreach efforts. Join us to make a difference.

Advisors

Our advisory board includes academics and industry experts who provide research insights and policy guidance.
